    • Getting a job!

    Who wants to hire a felon? There are actually a lot of places that are not only willing to hire a felon, but are looking for someone just like you to come and work. Or do you want to finish that education you started while you were in prison? Below you will find companies, colleges, and many other resources to help you with your search for a job or continuing your education.
