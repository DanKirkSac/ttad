    I am running for the Tahoe Truckee Airport District Board because I believe more can be done to enhance the safety of our communities to prevent and fight forest fires, run our airport efficiently and with a focus noise reduction, and to provide for community through STEM and other education programs.

    Fire Safety & prevention

    When it comes to preventing fires, and fighting them, our airport is the best tool we have as a community. We need to do more to bring our airport into readiness to support air operations in finding small fires before the grow, and in quickly attacking fires.
